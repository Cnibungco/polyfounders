Our Story

Founded in January 2012, Poly Founders was created with the purpose of connecting young entrepreneurs of all backgrounds under a common roof. While Cal Poly Pomona is our home, we welcome students, alumni, and professionals from all universities and organizations. Poly Founders works with budding entrepreneurs, exceptional students, and prominent professors in order to unite the worlds of entrepreneurship, academia, and industry.We develop entrepreneurs by offering the resources, knowledge, and network they need without oversight.
