WHAT IS POLY FOUNDERS?

Poly Founders- A multidisciplinary student organization based at Cal Poly Pomona that encourages entrepreneurship and innovation through by providing our members with the knowledge, network, and resources to change the world. We also organize some of the biggest innovative and groundbreaking events at Cal Poly Pomona including HackPoly and the Bronco Startup Challenge. We meet every Thursday at U-hour(12-1PM) in Building 163-1010.

Learn

We teach you the basics for what it takes to be an entrepreneur. From starting your business from the ground up to pitching to investors to finding the ideal co-founders, we have the resources to help.

Network

Your Network is your Net Worth. We at Poly Founders have a vast network of CEO's, Founders, Entrepreneurs, VP's of Fortune 500 companies and Investors. Ask us, we will connect the dots for you.

Innovate

Be disruptive. We organize Hackpoly, a 24 hour event where students from all over get together and collaboratively create a product like an app or website, and go on to pitch to successful entrepreneurs and executives.

Compete

Have the next million dollar idea? Come to our annual Bronco Startup Challenge where we have over $10,000 to give out.
