We are a group of entrepreneurs

Our story began in 2012

Poly Founders was created with the purpose of connecting young entrepreneurs of all backgrounds under a common roof. While Cal Poly Pomona is our home, we welcome students, alumni, and professionals from all universities and organizations. Poly Founders works with budding entrepreneurs, exceptional students, and prominent professors in order to unite the worlds of entrepreneurship, academia, and industry. We help entrepreneurs by offering our resources, knowledge, and network.

Learn

Life is about learning new things. In this case, we teach you the basics of what it takes to be an entrepreneur, pitching your ideas, as well as many other awesome things.

Do

Participate in our club activities and events. We host workshops and big events where major prizes are involved.

Hack

It's not about what you know, but who you know. We at Poly Founders have a large network of professionals who will potentially get you to where you want to be.

Network

We host two of Cal Poly Pomona's largest events: Bronco Startup Challenge and Hackpoly. Compete and win prizes and money. Not only that, learn new things and make new friends in the process!
