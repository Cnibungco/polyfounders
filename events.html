HackPoly
Hackpoly is Cal Poly Pomona's annual collegiate hackathon put on by Poly Founders. It is a 24-hour software and hardware competition to build anything.
Bronco Startup Challenge
Bronco Startup Challenge is Cal Poly Pomona's annual event put on by Poly Founders. It's a 2-month business building competition that rewards students with over $10,000 in cash.
